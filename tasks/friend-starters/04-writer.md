## What to send them

> Hey! Thanks for picking Writer. Here's your first 15-minute task.
>
> **Goal:** Rewrite the **welcome screen text** for Astrion OS. It's the first thing users see. Make it not-boring.
>
> **The current welcome text is:**
> > "Welcome to Astrion OS"
> > "An AI-native operating system with 52 apps, native C shell, and real bootable Linux."
>
> That's fine but kind of corporate. I want yours to feel like **a weird, confident, slightly funny 12-year-old made it.** Because I did.
>
> **Rules:**
> 1. Max 2 sentences.
> 2. Must mention that it **thinks** or is an **"operating system that thinks"**.
> 3. Must NOT use the words "revolutionary", "cutting-edge", "innovative", or "game-changer" — those are all dead.
> 4. Can be funny. Can be serious. Can be mysterious. Your call.
> 5. Must make me want to click "Continue".
>
> **Examples of the vibe I'm going for:**
> - "Astrion OS — a computer that actually listens. Welcome to the weird one."
> - "This computer tries to figure out what you want. Sometimes it's right."
> - "An operating system built by a 12-year-old who thought macOS was too polite."
>
> Write me 3 options. I'll pick the best one (or steal bits from all three).
