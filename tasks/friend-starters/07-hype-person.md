# 📣 Hype Person — Starter Task

## What to send them

> Hey! Thanks for picking Hype Person. Here's your first 15-minute task.
>
> **Goal:** Post ONE thing about Astrion OS on any social platform you use (Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, Discord, group chat — wherever you actually post).
>
> **What the post should do:**
> 1. **Show something cool about Astrion** — a screenshot, a short screen recording, or a link to the GitHub page
> 2. **Mention that a 12-year-old is building it** — that's the hook, it makes people actually click
> 3. **Include the link** — https://github.com/viraajbindra-a11y/Astrion-OS
>
> **Example caption you can steal:**
> > "my friend Viraaj is 12 and is building a real operating system from scratch, with AI baked in. it literally boots on a USB stick. i've been beta testing it and it's kinda insane. 
> > 
> > https://github.com/viraajbindra-a11y/Astrion-OS"
>
> Or if you're on TikTok and want to make it a video: literally just record your screen while booting the ISO in a VM. 30 seconds. Caption it "12 year old built this OS" and watch it do numbers.
>
> **You don't need my permission to post.** Post whatever feels authentic to your voice. The weirder the better.
>
> **Send me a screenshot of the post after** so I can see it and thank you properly.

## What Viraaj does with the result

1. Screenshot their post and save to `tasks/hype/` (create the folder)
2. Reply/react to the post if it feels right
3. **CHECK THE GITHUB STARS / TRAFFIC** in the repo insights — if their post drove clicks, it's working, ask them to do more
4. Ask them to do one post per release. Once they see the stars go up from their post, they'll keep doing it.

## Follow-up tasks

- Get them to make a 30-second demo video
- Ask them to tag you in a post so their audience sees your profile
- If they have a bigger following, ask them to do a longer "day-in-the-life of a 12-year-old building an OS" type post
- Pay them with feature names or app credits — "this feature was named by <name>" goes a long way socially
