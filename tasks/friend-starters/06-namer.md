# 🏷️ Namer — Starter Task

## What to send them

> Hey! Thanks for picking Namer. Here's your first 15-minute task.
>
> **Goal:** Come up with **5 name options** for Astrion's next 3 big things that still don't have good names:
>
> **1. The AI assistant.**
> Right now it's just called "Astrion" or "the AI." I need a real character name. Something memorable, a little weird, one syllable preferred, shouldn't sound like a corporate chatbot.
>
> Bad examples: Alex, Max, Buddy, Helper
> Good vibe: Nox, Juno, Vex, Ren, Kiva, Pilot, Ember
>
> Give me 5 options.
>
> **2. The Intent Kernel's "goal language."**
> In a few months Astrion will let you write little scripts in a tiny language for things like "organize my downloads every morning." I need a name for that language. It should feel small and powerful.
>
> Examples of real language names for inspiration: Ruby, Rust, Pika, Whisper, Flow, Grain
>
> Give me 5 options.
>
> **3. The marketplace where friends share scripts.**
> When the goal language ships, there'll be a marketplace where people share their scripts. I need a name for the marketplace.
>
> Bad examples: "The Store", "Astrion Hub", "Scripts.io"
> Good vibe: something that makes you curious
>
> Give me 5 options.
>
> **Total: 15 name ideas from you.** Don't overthink. First instincts are usually the best ones.

## What Viraaj does with the result

1. Read all 15 ideas
2. Pick the top 1-2 from each category
3. Sit with them for a day before committing
4. **The winners get used in the actual product.** That means their name lives inside Astrion OS forever.
5. Credit them in commit messages when you use their name (`git commit -m "Name AI assistant after <name>'s suggestion"`)

## Follow-up task

There's always something to name. Keep them on retainer. Once they do a few, they'll have a feel for "the Astrion voice" and their hit rate will go up.
